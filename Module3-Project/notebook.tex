\subsection{Emotion Detection and
Recognition}\label{emotion-detection-and-recognition}}

Emotion Detection and Recognition from text is closely related to
Sentiment Analysis which aims to detect and recognize various types of
feelings through the expression of texts, such as anger, disgust, fear,
happiness, sadness, and surprise unlike sentiment analysis which detects
positive, neutral, or negative feelings from text.

\item
  Perform any preprocessing that may be required
\end{itemize}
